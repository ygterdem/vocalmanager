Karaoke 🎤
Original short pieces with lyrics and a chord-pad backing. Pick a track, sing the words as the notes scroll past, and the app scores your pitch. Quiet rooms work best — the pad is intentionally low so it doesn't drown your voice.
Import your own song
Drop a chart JSON (notes + lyrics + chords) and optionally an audio file to use as the backing track instead of the chord pad. Schema documented at the top of renderer/karaoke.js. Imported songs stay loaded until you close the app.
